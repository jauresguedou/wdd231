Color Schema

To give our trendy shoes website a luxury, premium, elegant vibe, we've selected the following key colors:

  • Primary Color: Deep Navy
    Usage: Gives a refined, sophisticated tone and replaced plain black with a more stylish, deep tone. Sets the luxurious atmosphere without being overwhelming. It will be used for the footer background , the primary branding elements , the nav bar background.
  • Secondary Color: Gold
    Usage: Communicates exclusivity, prestige, and high value. Draws attention to key actions without overwhelming the page. I will be used to highlight elements like "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now" buttons. Small icons, underlines, or borders. Hover effects for links or call to actions.
  • Accent Color: Cream White
    Usage: Adds a soft, classy contrast to the dark tones, keeps the design from feeling too harsh while maintaining elegance. I will be used for section background for products highlights or testimonials, button text or icons when placed over dark backgrounds, border for product cards or modals.
  • Background Color: Off-Black
    Usage: Creates depth and elegance , provides a sleek canvas that makes products and images pop, improves focus by reducing visual noise. It will be used for overall site background , product listing sections , hero sections behind featured images.
  • Text Color: Warm Gray
    Usage: Easy to read on dark backgrounds without the harshness of pure white , retains a premium look while improving accessibility. It will be used for body text , product descriptions , footer text , secondary UI labels

Typography

The typography section of the planning document provides examples of the fonts to be used and where they are to be applied. For this design, we have selected two key fonts:

  • Montserrat – To be used for all headings (h1, h2), logos, and key navigational elements. This font provides a modern and bold presentation.
  • Roboto – To be used for body text, paragraphs, and product descriptions to ensure readability and a clean, contemporary look.
